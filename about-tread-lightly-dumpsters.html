Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any prohibited items I can't put in a roll off dumpster?

Yes, certain hazardous materials such as chemicals, batteries, asbestos, and tires are not allowed in roll off dumpsters. Tread Lightly Dumpsters can provide a complete list of prohibited items.

Can I rent a dumpster for yard waste removal?

Yes, Tread Lightly Dumpsters offers roll off dumpsters that are perfect for yard waste removal. This includes branches, grass clippings, and leaves. These dumpsters are ideal for landscaping projects or large cleanups.

What are the most common uses for roll off dumpsters?

Roll off dumpsters are commonly used for a variety of projects, including home renovations, large cleanouts, construction waste, and yard debris removal. Tread Lightly Dumpsters provides services for all these needs.

How much does it cost to rent a roll off dumpster?

The cost depends on the size of the dumpster, the rental duration, and your location. Tread Lightly Dumpsters provides transparent pricing with no hidden fees. You can contact them for a customized quote.

What happens if I need my dumpster picked up early?

If you complete your project ahead of schedule, you can contact Tread Lightly Dumpsters to arrange an early pickup. They offer flexible scheduling to accommodate changes in your timeline.
