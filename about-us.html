About us – Tread Lightly Dumpsters

Clint and Kelli Starks founded Tread Lightly Dumpsters to offer dependable and cost-effective waste disposal services for residential and commercial project needs. Tim, Kelli, and the team put forth a lot of effort to go above and beyond what customers expect by offering helpful support, punctual delivery, and practical, flexible scheduling options. We're dedicated to always being the people our friends, family, and neighbors can depend on.

Whatever your waste removal needs, we recognize that your time and money are valuable. As a result, we work hard to provide simple, cost-effective solutions, whether you're planning a home renovation or overseeing a commercial roof replacement. Let us handle the labor-intensive tasks so you can concentrate on the more essential aspects of your project or job site. For more information on your options, get in touch with our team or view our online container size guide to learn more.
