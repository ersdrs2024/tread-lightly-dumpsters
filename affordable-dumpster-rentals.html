How do I schedule a dumpster drop-off?

Scheduling a dumpster drop-off with Tread Lightly Dumpsters is simple. You can call their office or use their online booking system. Once scheduled, the dumpster will be delivered to your specified location.

What services does Tread Lightly Dumpsters offer for commercial projects?

Tread Lightly Dumpsters provides roll off dumpster rentals for a variety of commercial projects, including construction, demolition, and office cleanouts. Their services are tailored to meet the needs of businesses.

What happens if I exceed the weight limit for my dumpster?

If you exceed the weight limit, there may be additional charges. Tread Lightly Dumpsters will inform you of the extra cost based on the overage. It’s recommended to monitor the weight to avoid unnecessary fees.

Does Tread Lightly Dumpsters offer recycling services?

Yes, Tread Lightly Dumpsters encourages recycling by ensuring that recyclable materials are properly handled. Items such as metals, cardboard, and certain plastics can be sorted and processed separately.
