Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any weight limits for roll off dumpsters?

Each dumpster size has a specific weight limit. It’s important to stay within these limits to avoid additional fees. Tread Lightly Dumpsters provides guidelines on weight limits for each size.

What is a roll off dumpster, and how is it used?

A roll off dumpster is a large, open-top container designed for waste disposal. It is often delivered to your site and used for projects like renovations, cleanouts, and construction debris removal. Tread Lightly Dumpsters offers a range of sizes, making it easier to choose one that suits your needs.

Do I need a permit to place a roll off dumpster on my street?

In some cases, a permit is required if the dumpster will be placed on public property such as streets or sidewalks. Tread Lightly Dumpsters can guide you through the process of obtaining the necessary permits if needed.
