Does Tread Lightly Dumpsters offer same-day service?

Is there a limit to how high I can fill the dumpster?

Yes, for safety reasons, dumpsters should not be overfilled. Waste should not extend above the top edge of the container. Overfilled dumpsters can lead to additional charges and potential hazards during transport.
