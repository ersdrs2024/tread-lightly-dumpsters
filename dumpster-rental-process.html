Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to rent a roll off dumpster?

The cost depends on the size of the dumpster, the rental duration, and your location. Tread Lightly Dumpsters provides transparent pricing with no hidden fees. You can contact them for a customized quote.

What happens if I need my dumpster picked up early?

If you complete your project ahead of schedule, you can contact Tread Lightly Dumpsters to arrange an early pickup. They offer flexible scheduling to accommodate changes in your timeline.

How quickly can I get a dumpster delivered?

Tread Lightly Dumpsters offers quick delivery, typically within 24 to 48 hours of your order. Same-day delivery may be available depending on your location and dumpster availability.

Can I change my dumpster size after ordering?

Yes, if your project scope changes, Tread Lightly Dumpsters can help you switch to a larger or smaller dumpster, depending on availability. Contact them to make the necessary adjustments to your order.

What should I consider when placing the dumpster on my property?

Ensure that the area is accessible for the delivery truck and that it does not block driveways or sidewalks. A flat, stable surface is ideal for the dumpster placement to avoid damage to your property.
