Frequently Asked Questions

What types of waste can I put in a roll off dumpster?

Tread Lightly Dumpsters allows most non-hazardous materials such as construction debris, household waste, and yard waste. However, hazardous materials like chemicals and certain electronics are not permitted.

How long can I keep the dumpster on my property?

The rental period typically lasts between 7 to 14 days, but longer rentals can be arranged. Tread Lightly Dumpsters is flexible and offers extended rental periods if needed.

What size dumpsters are available for rental?

Tread Lightly Dumpsters offers various dumpster sizes, ranging from small 10-yard options to larger 40-yard containers. These sizes accommodate different project scales, from small cleanups to large construction jobs.
