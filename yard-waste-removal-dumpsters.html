Are there any weight limits for roll off dumpsters?

Each dumpster size has a specific weight limit. It’s important to stay within these limits to avoid additional fees. Tread Lightly Dumpsters provides guidelines on weight limits for each size.

What is a roll off dumpster, and how is it used?

A roll off dumpster is a large, open-top container designed for waste disposal. It is often delivered to your site and used for projects like renovations, cleanouts, and construction debris removal.
